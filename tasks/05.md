- In the consistency heatmap, **DO NOT** visually differentiate future dates from past dates without activity. All cells without workout data should use the same style (`border border-border`), regardless of whether they are past or future dates.

The consistency heatmap should display the **last 3 months** (current month + 2 previous months), using actual calendar weeks (Monday to Sunday) as columns, grouped by month.
